Our Story

A Garden Retreat
Beneath the Kilimanjaro Sky

Nestled at the foot of Mount Kilimanjaro in Kimana, Jacaranda Garden Restaurant is where the wilderness meets warmth. Blooming jacaranda trees frame every meal, and the crisp highland air adds a flavour no kitchen can replicate.

We serve an ever-changing menu of local and continental dishes, brewed Kenyan teas, and char-grilled specialities — around the clock, because great hunger has no schedule.
